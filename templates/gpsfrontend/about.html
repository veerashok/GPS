About Us

A Few words about us

Group of five concerned friends, who were worried about the medium of education. They were worried about the future of children those who were lacking good quality English education. All of them are well placed in their life so they could have left the topic after gossiping for a while but they have decided to make a difference by doing something concrete. They have dared to take the problem heads on and they have decided to start a new school with crowdfunding and their own sources. They have roamed to almost all villages to Chohtan block to arrange funding for the school. There were so many obstacles but convication, determination and will power of them have given birth to a beautiful school called Genius Public School. We are a non-profit school run by a scoiety called Meghmaya Foundation. We are registered under the SOCIETIES REGISTRATION ACT, 1860. We are partially funded by crowdfuding.

Welcome!

We welcome you to prestigious and one of the first English medium school of our town Chohtan. We are fully determined and dedicated to make our town's education as par as metropolitan cities.

Our Mission

We aim to provide word class education to our students. We had started this school to shape future of our children and make them world leaders. We give personal care to every student. We believe that every child of this world has some special abilities and talents. We try to find out all such abilities and talents through our excellent teaching environment and education.
