We welcome you to prestigious and one of the first English medium school of our town Chohtan. We are fully determined and dedicated to make our town's education as par as metropolitan cities.

Our Mission

We aim to provide word class education to our students. We had started this school to shape future of our children and make them world leaders. We give personal care to every student. We believe that every child of this world has some special abilities and talents. We try to find out all such abilities and talents through our excellent teaching environment and education.
